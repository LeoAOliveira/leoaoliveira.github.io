Hefesto

Testing with Paralympic athletes!

On september 2019, the Hefesto development team, composed by Victor Falcetta, Luiza Fattori and myself, tested the app in the Brazilian Paralympic Training Center. Aline Rocha, a successful Brazilian athlete, and her team tested Hefesto in an athletics track.
